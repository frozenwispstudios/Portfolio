Across Kingdoms (VR)

Across Kingdoms was a student project that 2 friends and I built in 6 weeks I handled programming the A.I, Creating the level design and U.I design, While my friends programmed the player, U.I and worked on polish like explosive barrels or death particles. This project was a solid build that with a little more time could become a complete project ready for sale. With lots of kingdoms to visit and protect from the hordes of enemies some human, some monsters shoot them all before they rip down your gates and destroy the crystals that link the worlds. Developed for Windows mixed reality headset.

Sinking Sailors

The Theme for Sinking Sailors was 'repair' for the global game jam. I programmed/designed obstacles like Rocks, Sharks, and so on while also work on the boat controls and sinking mechanic. My friends worked on Player and 3D art. To keep your boat from sinking you and some co-op friends of up to 4 need to repair all the damages done to the boat, fix the sails, hammer the holes and steer the boat to victory.

How to make friends

How to make friends was a project me and a friend made for the NZ game jam the theme was chain reaction. We both wanted to make something new and in a different engine to what we had made before while also tackling new tasks. So, my friends worked on the players while I programmed all the A.I, in this case, we have a combat system that both the AI and players shared. While there are also 3 different A.I types. One is the standard grunt one another was a bigger slower heavier hitting unit and the last was fast and skinny. The summary is that it's a local multiplayer game where each player must build an army to take over the flag like the king of the hill with the players giving orders like get more people for the army or come to me or go attack that location. This trailer was edited by Min-Su Lee check him out here : https://minwater2.github.io/
